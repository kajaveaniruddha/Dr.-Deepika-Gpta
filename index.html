About me

Deepika Gupta is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at IIIT-Vadodara International Campus Diu. Before joining this esteemed academic institution, she worked as a Senior Software Engineer at Samsung Research Institute in Delhi for a period of four and a half years where she gained valuable industry experience and honed her skills in software development.Her contributions to the field of Computer Science have been significant, driven by her research interests in Document Image Processing, Image Processing, Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Pattern Recognition.

She received her Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from the prestigious IIT (ISM) Dhanbad in the year 2021.Prior to her Ph.D.,she completed her M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from MNIT Jaipur in 2011 and B.Tech from Uttar Pradesh Technical University in 2008.Through her academic and professional accomplishments,she has demonstrated an outstanding level of expertise in her chosen field.
